
ARCADE FİSH
Well, since I'm a fan of old-school games, why not make a classic arcade game but with fish as the characters?

You have four characters to choose from: A "fish" that can shoot water bombs; a "carp" that can use his strength to break obstacles; a shark who can eat the other characters, and a eel who is the fastest character with weak attacks.

You can design levels like: "going for food", "escaping from a net" or even fighting the final boss, a fisherman.


FISH CHESS
Core Concept
An aquatic twist on chess where living fish pieces swim around in a 3D aquarium-board, adding dynamic movement and special abilities to traditional chess strategy.
Unique Elements

    Living Board: Chessboard is a sectioned aquarium with different depths and coral decorations
    Swimming Pieces:

    King: Majestic Lionfish
    Queen: Graceful Angelfish
    Bishop: Quick Marlins
    Knight: Seahorses
    Rook: Sturdy Groupers
    Pawns: Small Clownfish



Special Mechanics

Power-ups:

    Water Bomb: Creates currents that can push pieces
    Poison Cloud: Temporarily weakens enemy pieces
    Feeding Time: Attracts pieces to certain squares
    Coral Shield: Temporary protection


Environmental Effects:

    Random currents affecting movement
    Bubble streams creating temporary barriers
    Changing water temperatures affecting piece speed



Visual Style

    Beautiful underwater chess environment
    Dynamic lighting and water effects
    Particle effects for special abilities
    Fish animations showing personality
